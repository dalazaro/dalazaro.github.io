About

Before I was a part of WDI 39, I was a junior high math and instrumental music teacher at a private K-8 school in the East Bay. With the support of my wonderful fianceé, I decided to switch to web developing because I've always wanted to build my own websites, and I would like to earn more money for my family in the long run.

Besides coding, I like to listen to vinyls with my fianceé, build Legos with my 3-year-old son, play acoustic and electric guitar, ride my single-speed bike, eat keto, watch professional wrestling, and hone my craft of coffeemaking via the Aeropress and cold brew methods.
