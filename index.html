About

I'm super excited to be a part of WDI 39 at General Assembly! A little bit about me: I'm a Bay Area native, born and raised in Hayward. I graduated from UC Davis in 2011 with a BA in English with a minor in Music, and sang and played guitar in my folk band [Tor House].

Just before WDI 39, I was a junior high math and instrumental music teacher at a private K-8 school in Fremont. With the support of my wonderful fianceé, I decided to switch to web developing because I've always wanted to build my own websites, and I would like to earn more money for my family in the long run.

Besides coding, I like to listen to vinyls with my fianceé, build Legos with my 3-year-old son, play acoustic and electric guitar, ride my single-speed bike, eat keto, watch professional wrestling, and hone my craft of coffeemaking via the Aeropress and cold brew methods.
