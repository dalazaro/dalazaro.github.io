Projects

peached.ink

Presented as capstone project for Web Development Immersive.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Bootstrap 4, Heroku, BCrypt

peached.ink is an online art portfolio for illustrator Kean Saetern. Visitors can view her illustration slideshows, read her profile, and connect with her. Behind the scenes, Kean can log in to the app through a hidden endpoint to access her custom CMS, update her profile, and CRUD her own illustrations.

Moviee

This app is a replication of Moviee, updated to resolve various issues in the original codebase.

Technologies: React, The Movie Database API, Heroku

Moviee is a React single-page application that displays recently released popular movie selections according to The Movie Database (TMDB) API. Users can also search the TMDB database for a particular movie.

Ponder

Served as creator of Ponder and project leader for a team of three developers.

Technologies: Ruby on Rails, PostgreSQL, Materialize, Digital Ocean, BCrypt

Ponder is a distraction-free microblogging app for creative writers, prioritizing the distribution of original content in the form of philosophies, poems, prose, or movie/music/TV show reviews.

Slurp SF

Technologies: Node.js/Express, MongoDB, JavaScript, AJAX, Bootstrap, Heroku

Slurp SF is a crowdsourced guide to ramen restaurants in San Francisco. Users can view a list of ramen shops in a specific neighborhood, and read food recommendations (called "slurps") written by others. Users may also add a restaurant to the database, or write their own slurps.

Geoquakes

Technologies: Google Maps, USGS GeoJSON API, JavaScript, JSON, AJAX, Bootstrap, GitHub Pages

This live earthquake feed application accesses data from the United States Geological Services API and renders time, magnitude, and location onto the page, along with location markers in a Google Map interface.
